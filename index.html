Student portal

Provide your students with a branded portal where they can access:

  • Curriculum & syllabus
  • LMS & class learning material
  • Attendances, class reports & class recordings
  • Assessments, grades and learning analytics
  • Access to classes (virtual classroom)
  • Payments, invoices & program fees
  • Chats (w/ teachers, administators & other students)

Teacher portal

Simplify your teacher recruiting, training, onboarding process with an intuitive teacher portal to access:

  • Student info
  • Curriculum & syllabus
  • Class learning material
  • Teaching methodology
